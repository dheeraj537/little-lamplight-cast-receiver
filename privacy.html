Little Lamplight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Little Lamplight is a bedtime reading app that helps parents read aloud with their children. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

The short version

Little Lamplight does not have user accounts, does not use advertising or analytics, and does not sell your data. Settings and reading progress stay on your device. When you use optional AI features (natural storyteller voices and the Reading Coach), text only is sent to OpenAI to generate audio or coaching feedback — never your voice recordings.

What stays on your device

  • Language and voice preferences
  • Reading garden / streak progress
  • Cached story audio files (so lines play faster after the first time)

Microphone & speech recognition

If you turn on Say it together (voice mode), use Copycat, or hold the button in Reading Coach, the app listens through your device's microphone. Audio is processed using your device's built-in speech recognition (Apple's Speech framework on iOS, Android's Speech Recognizer). Little Lamplight does not record, store, or transmit raw microphone audio to our servers. Depending on your device and settings, some speech recognition may be processed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.

Voice features are optional. Microphone access is only requested when you use a feature that needs it.

Natural storyteller voices (OpenAI text-to-speech)

When you tap Listen first, hear Jugnu's prompts, or use natural voices while casting, the written text of that line is sent to OpenAI to generate spoken audio. The resulting audio is cached on your device. OpenAI's privacy policy governs their handling of that text.

Reading Coach (OpenAI chat)

When you use the Reading Coach, the target sentence on the page and, if available, a text transcript of what the device heard you read are sent to OpenAI to generate a short encouragement message. Your voice is never uploaded — only text.

If you are offline or AI is unavailable, the app shows a kind offline tip instead.

Casting to a TV

If you cast to a Chromecast, the current page's text and illustration are sent over your local network to your TV. When natural voices are enabled, generated audio may also be streamed to the TV from your phone. No personal account information is included.

What we don't do

  • No account creation or sign-in
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking SDKs
  • No sale of personal information

Children's privacy

Little Lamplight is designed to be used by a parent or caregiver together with their child, not by a child independently. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
