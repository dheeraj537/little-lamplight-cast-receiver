Little Lamplight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Little Lamplight is a bedtime reading app that helps parents read aloud with their children. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

The short version

Little Lamplight does not have user accounts, does not collect personal information, does not use analytics or advertising, and does not send your data to any server we operate. Everything the app remembers (your language choice, reading streak, settings) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Microphone & speech recognition

If you turn on "Say it together" (voice mode), the app listens through your device's microphone while you're on a story page, to detect when you and your child say that page's word aloud. This audio is processed using your device's built-in speech recognition (Apple's Speech framework on iOS, Android's Speech Recognizer on Android) — Little Lamplight does not record, store, or transmit this audio anywhere itself. Depending on your device and settings, some audio may be processed by Apple or Google's speech recognition services under their own respective privacy policies. Voice mode is off by default and only activates while you're actively on a story page with it enabled.

Casting to a TV

If you cast a story to a Chromecast device, the current page's text and illustration reference are sent over your local network to your TV so it can display the story. No personal information is included in this, and nothing is sent to us or stored outside your local network.

What we don't do

  • No account creation or sign-in
  • No collection of your or your child's personal information
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No data sold or shared with third parties

Children's privacy

Little Lamplight is designed to be used by a parent or caregiver together with their child, not by a child independently. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
